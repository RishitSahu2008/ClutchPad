Clutchpad uses kmk based on circuitpython to map the switchpress to the desired task. I have assignes the following - 
- The roatary encoder controls the volume.
- The keymap is as follows :
  
| C1 | C2 | C3 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Q | W | E |
| A | S | D |
| Z | R | C |
- OLED displays - 'Ready to clutch'. You may change it if you like.
